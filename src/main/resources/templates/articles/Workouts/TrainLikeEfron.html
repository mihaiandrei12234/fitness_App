The High School Musical Trilogy really laid the foundation for Efron’s career and he’s had steady work ever since in a variety of roles as he’s diversified himself in Hollywood.

Since, he’s become a fitspiration for a lot of people with his Instagram workout posts and his YouTube series “Gym Time”.

Zac Efron Inspired Workout Overview

The following workout routine is inspired by the way Zac Efron trains and the content on the web about Efron’s training. It is not the actual workout he has performed and isn’t a representation of Patrick Murphy’s programs either.

It has, however, been put together with sound training principles and includes a lot of examples of the supersets he might incorporate on specific training days.

The workout can be performed for 12 weeks. During those 12 weeks you will perform the workouts in a 3 days on 1 day off fashion, starting over with the first workout listed after your rest day. This sort of training frequency will help you build lean muscle mass.

Keep your rest periods relatively short throughout each workout. 45-60 seconds in between supersets should do the trick.

Letters represent that the exercise is part of a pairing that forms a superset and/or circuit. Perform all of the exercises of the pairing prior to taking your rest period.

After each workout, perform 20 minutes of a cardio of your choice. It doesn’t have to be too strenuous and it should be something you enjoy doing as this will make you consistent in performing it.

Weight selection will be highly individualized. You will want to select a weight that challenges you during both exercises.
